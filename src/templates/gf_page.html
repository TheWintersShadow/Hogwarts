Challenge 3:

Solve the collatz conjecture for the numbers 50,000 to 50,500. Find any reductions that reach the 222th step (counting like a programmer) and save the number at that step. The flag is these numbers appended together in a string. For example, for numbers 0-100 reduced that reach step 111: 73 reduces to 8 at the 111th step; 97 reduces to 20 at the 111th step. No other numbers make it to this step. Therefore the flag would be 820.
